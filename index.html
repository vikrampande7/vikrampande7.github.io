About Me

I am Vikram, from Pune, India. I am a master's student at NC State University in the Electrical and Computer Engineering Department with a focus in Machine Learning. I have completed my Bachelor's degree in Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering from the University of Pune. Before joining NC State, I was working as an Associate Software Engineer at Accenture, India in the domains of ML/NLP and Backend development. My research interests include AI - fusion of Vision and Language, Multimodal AI, Generative AI, Large Foundation Models, and Explainable AI.

In my spare time, I enjoy watching movies and anime. I also have a passion for playing and watching Football. Additionally, I write about AI and Data Science, and you can find my blogs here.

Experience

I am currently working as a Data Science Consultant with Data and Visualization Services at NC State University where I provide consultations to students, researchers and faculty from other departments on Python, Machine Learning and GIT. (Jan 23 - Present)

In addition, I volunteer at ARoS Lab where I contribute to research focused on detecting and classifying damaged and healthy leaves of St. Augastine turfgrass. For this project, I am implementing object detection and image segmentation algorithms with Deep Learning techniques. (Jan 23 - Present)

Previously, I worked as an Associate Software Engineer at Accenture, India. In this role, I was involved in building, developing and testing ML/NLP models, including classification, recommendation systems, Named Entity Recognition (NER), and text-summarization. I also had responsibilities for creating ML APIs with Django and Python, and deploying them with Docker and Kubernetes on Azure cloud platform. Additionally, I worked in backend development using C#, .NET and MicrosoftSQLServer. (May 21 - June 22)
